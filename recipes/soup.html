Soup Recipe

Hot Soup! (but really at whatever temperature you prefer)

You may not be able to see yourself in it, but you can see it in you!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • I mean, back in the day they would make this with rocks ... any other non-water ingredient(s) will do!

Instructions

  1. Boil up that water (if you're using rocks, you'll wanna get this really hot ...)
  2. Toss in everything you've got
  3. Find a comically large implement to stir with (rock soup won't delight your guests, might as well throw in some comedy)
  4. Cook for as long as your heart desires (I've heard some of the best spots have been cooking out of the same batch for a grip ...)
